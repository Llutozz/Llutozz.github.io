Project Summary

Rocket Rider is an action game that fuses platforming with intense hack & slash combat. The game is set in a dystopian futuristic city heavily controlled by the authorities that oppress the citizens with unending curfews. The player assumes the role of John Rider, a rocket-riding rebel, too stubborn and knowledgeable to bend to the regime. His goal? To ignite the spark of rebellion, dismantle the enforcers' armies, and free the city!

Project Information

Team Size: 17

Duration: 9 Weeks

Engine: Unreal Engine 5
